Pasilyo - SunKissed Lola
"Pasilyo" (transl.Aisle) is a song recorded by Filipino band SunKissed Lola. It was released on October 28, 2022, by Ditto Music as their third single overall. The song was written by Alvin Serito and collaboratively produced by the band members. Lead vocalist Dan Ombao explained that the concept of the song is to live the dream of being married someday and being someone's certainty.
